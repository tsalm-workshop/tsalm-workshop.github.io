**Arjun Ashok** is a PhD Student at the University of Montreal and Mila-Quebec AI Institute, advised by Irina Rish and Alexandre Drouin. He is also a Visiting Researcher at ServiceNow Research, Montreal, Canada. He is the co-creator of the Lag-Llama foundation model for forecasting and the Context-is-Key benchmark for context-aided forecasting. His research interests lie in time series forecasting and decision-making, with a focus on designing scalable general-purpose models for time series prediction tasks. He has given multiple talks on his work on flexible time series prediction models and time series foundation models. He was a co-organizer of the "Time Series in the Age of Large Models" workshop at NeurIPS 2024. He serves as a regular reviewer for machine learning and forecasting conferences and has been recognized as a top reviewer.
